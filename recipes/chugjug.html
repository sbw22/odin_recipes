Description

The Chug Jug, the greatest drink in Fortnite Battle Royale! Containing 5 gallons of slurp juice (roughly), the Chug Jug ways 43 pounds when full and gives 100 health points and 100 sheild points. The Chug Jug has 645.5 calories. For saftey reasons, since slurp juice is made of toxic sludge, we will use kool aid instead. Also, The following recipe was taken from @Lennie on food.com.

Ingredients

  • 5 Gallon Barrel
  • 15 (6 grams) packages of blue kool aid
  • 7.5 grames of sugar
  • 5 gallons of cold water.

Ingredients

  1. Stir together all of the kool aid and sugar in the barrel.
  2. Add about 1/3 of the water. Make sure the sugar and water dissolve.
  3. Slowly stir in the rest of the water.
  4. Enjoy!
