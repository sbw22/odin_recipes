Golden Apple

Home Page

Description

The Golden Apple, one of the best foods in Minecraft! Weighs 7.5 ounces, 116 calories, costing around $29.00, gives four hunger points, and gives Absorption I and Regeneration II!

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • (At least) 220 square cm of edible gold foil.

Steps

  1. Wash apple.
  2. Completly cover apple with as much edible gold foil as needed.
  3. Enjoy!
