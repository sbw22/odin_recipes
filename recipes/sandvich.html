Sandvich

Home Page

Decription

The Sandvich, the greatest food in TF2! Weighs 4 ounces, 200 calories, 1.5 inches tall, and +120 nutritional health points!

Ingredients

  • 2 Diagnally cut Bread Slices
  • Ham
  • Swiss Cheese
  • 4 Tomato Slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tooth Pick
  • olive

Steps

  1. Layer the ingredients mentioned in this step in the order provided: bread slice, new meat, swiss cheese, all four tomato slices, lettuce, bread slice.
  2. Stick half of the tooth pick in the middle and top of the sandvich.
  3. Stick the olive on the end of the tooth pick.
  4. Enjoy!
